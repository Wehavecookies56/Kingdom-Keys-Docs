Keyblades

Keyblades are sword-like weapons that are in the shape of a key. There 120 different keyblades available in the mod, many of which have 3D models. They are summoned by using their respective keychain. They can open chests from the mod and iron doors. Every keyblade is unbreakable with -1 durability. The stats can be changed of every keyblade in the config file.

Summoning a keyblade

To summon a keyblade the respective keychain must be placed in the keychain inventory found in the menu (default 'M'). Then with an empty hand press the summon key (default 'G'). The keyblade then can be desummoned by pressing the key again.
